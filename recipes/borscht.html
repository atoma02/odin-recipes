Borscht

Description

Borscht is a sour soup commonly made using beet root

Ingredients

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 3 beets
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 cup of beans
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel, chop, and slice vegetables
  2. Boil broth and water in a large pot
  3. Once potatoes soften, add beans, bay leaves, and salt
  4. Simmer for 3 min and serve
